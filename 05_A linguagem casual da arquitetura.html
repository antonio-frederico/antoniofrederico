Antônio Frederico Lasalvia

A linguagem casual da arquitetura

2021

This was the sui-generis occasion to join my studies in philosophy, dance and architecture into a single theoretical work: a research which inquiries into the role chance plays on the perennial change of the built environment through the lens of Contact Improvisation.

Towards the end of my Master’s, I was lucky enough to interview some thoughtful figures within contemporary discourse of the addressed fields, such as Steve Paxton (in dance), Anne Lacaton & Jean Phillip Vassal, Georges Descombes and Carla Juaçaba (in architecture). Their responses can be found on the link below (english translation at the end of the document p.310-318).
